How Mentoring Works

Getting started with mentoring is simple and straightforward. Follow these easy steps to connect with your mentor.

Browse Mentors

Explore available mentors and use filters to find someone who matches your needs.

View Profile

Open a mentor profile to learn about their background, interests, and mentorship focus.

Request Mentor

Follow the instructions to send a mentoring request via email to the student representative.

Start Mentoring

Begin your mentoring relationship for the semester and connect with your mentor.

Mentorship typically lasts one semester and can be extended by mutual agreement.

About the Mentoring Program

Our mentors are students who have already completed at least one semester and volunteer to help new or incoming students navigate university life.

Mentoring is informal, supportive, and flexible. There is no obligation â€” either side can stop anytime. This is about building connections and making your transition to university smoother and more welcoming.

Whether you're a first-year student, international student, or just looking for guidance, our mentors are here to help you feel at home.
